| MAC & CHEESE |

Preperation Time: 10 minutes | Cooking Time: 45 minutes | Total Time: 55 minutes

Ingredients:

  • 2 1/2 cups elbow macaroni
  • 1/4 cup butter
  • 1/4 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 4 cups milk
  • 500g shredded cheddar cheese
  • 1/4 cup butter, melted
  • 1 1/2 cup breadcrumbs

Directions:

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F
  2. Bring a large pot of salted water to a boil. Cook macaroni till al dente (~7-8 mins.)
  3. Melt butter, then whisk in flour, ensuring there are no lumps. Cook for 2-3 mins.
  4. Whisk in milk, cooking till mixture is slightly thick (6 mins).
  5. Turn off heat, add cheddar cheese gradually and mix till melted. Stir in macaroni.
  6. Add to a baking dish. Combine breadcrumbs with melted butter, and add to the top of dish till covered.
  7. Bake till golden brown, ~30 mins
